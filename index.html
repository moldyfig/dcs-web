Our next program


October 29 & 30, 2023

Sunday and Monday

7:30 p.m.


Shepley Bulfinch

3443 N. Central, at Osborn

Phoenix

Featuring Beethoven's String Quartet Op. 95 Serioso and music from the film There Will Be Blood by composer Jonny Greenwood of Radiohead which depicts the gritty early years of the US oil prospecting business. The concerts will be in the epic mid-century modern building by W.A. Sarmiento at Central and Osborn, hosted by the architectural firm Shepley Bulfinch, amidst an exhibition of photographs and architectural projects.

Please note: The Sunday, Oct. 29 concert is sold out. If this is the only night you can attend, please call 602-254-1491 and leave a message. We will call you back promptly if seats become available.

Tickets $25. Join us!
