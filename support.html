Support the Series

With no paid staff and very low overhead, a donation to The Downtown Chamber Series travels a great distance, helping us do everything from paying stipends to the musicians and art spaces to renting chairs and staging. Our skilled volunteers, loyal and growing audience, and your donations have collectively kept our ticket prices the lowest in town. Thus, our concerts are accessible to all, including full-time students who can attend for free. Thank you!

Volunteer

Downtown Chamber Series volunteers are the backbone of this concert series. Our events function like a MASH unit: all equipment has to be rented and setup in the art spaces where we perform. These tasks include hauling equipment to the venue, setting up chairs and lights, and food and drink prep for the reception. At the concerts, volunteers manage the box office and run the reception. In return for your help, you get a free concert and recognition in the program. Interested? Great! Call us at 602-254-1491 and leave a message. We will follow up!
