Volunteer

Downtown Chamber Series volunteers are the backbone of this concert series. Our events function like a MASH unit: all equipment has to be installed in the art spaces where we perform. These tasks include hauling equipment to the venue and setting up chairs and lights. At the concerts, volunteers manage the box office and run the reception. In return for your help, you get a free concert and recognition in the program. Interested? Great! Call us at 602-254-1491 and leave a message. We will follow up!
