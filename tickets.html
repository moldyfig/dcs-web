Directions & Parking

The Abbey
302 W. Monroe at 3rd Ave.
Phoenix, AZ 85003

The Abbey does not have a designated free parking lot. However, here is a list of suggested parking options:

  • On the NE corner of Monroe and 3rd Ave, just East of The Abbey, the parking garage in The X Phoenix has hourly parking at $6/hr, with a max rate of $22. This is the most convenient option.
  • There is metered street parking on Monroe and surrounding streets, as available.
  • There are several pay lots nearby; however, their rates are priced dynamically from $20 to $40.
  • There is street parking in the neighborhoods around Filmore and Roosevelt Streets North of Van Buren. Consider leaving your car parked there and taking a short ride share with Lyft or Uber to The Abbey.
